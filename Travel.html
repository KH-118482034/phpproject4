My Travel Experiences

London

I regularly travel to London to visit my brother who lives over there. I love to do bus tours of the city. There is something there for everyone. From listening to the bells of Big Ben, looking over the Thames River from the London eye to getting a glimpse of the well guarded Buckingham Palace.

Moreover, the nightlife is superb. Over Christmas, I went to the nightclub Fabric which has the best DJ's in the city. The culture, in addition, to the delicious food is why I love to visit the city. On my final day, I went on a truck which is also a boat. This was a unique experience for me and opened my eyes to the wonderful inventions around us.

Bali

Bali is situated in Indonesia. My family and I spent 2 weeks in this beautiful island. The blue skies and crystal clear waters are spectacular. On the first day, we embarked on a safari which went from the mountains right down to the sea. A four wheel jeep brought us through the landscapes and we got to see the animals in their natural habitats. The orangutan's and the lions were my favourite animals.

The next morning, we got up at dawn to see sunrise and to embark on hike up the forested volcanic mountains. From the top of the mountain we got to see the religious cliffside Uluwatu Temple. Lastly, we enjoyed the art market and water sports such as scuba diving. The expenses and the time it took us to reach Bali was definitely worth it!

Portugal

My family and I spent 3 weeks last summer in the Algarve. The beaches in addition to the food and nightlife were brilliant. The nightlife was electric and the landscapes were beautiful.
